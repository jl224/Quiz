Javascript Coding Quiz

Quiz Instructions: The quiz consists of 5 questions and you have 90 seconds to complete the quiz. Once the start button is pushed, the timer will initialize the countdown. For every question answered incorrectly, 5 seconds will be deducted from the countdown. The timer stops when all questions are answered and the "Submit Answers" button has been pushed. Once the answers are submitted, the score will be displayed at the bottom of the page. Questions answered correctly will be highlighted in green. Questions answered incorrectly will be highlighted in red. Good luck!
